# NEW COLSTAN

My first modded UNCIV civilization. Slightly overpowered and meant to be a fun 'what-if' civ.

Starts with Industrialization and a few industrial-era techs unlocked. Its up to you to research the rest and reindustrialize your civilization.
Conquer the world before they can get ahead, promote your advanced culture and wield economic might, or invest in science and reach ever-greater heights
